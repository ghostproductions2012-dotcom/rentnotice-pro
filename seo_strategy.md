# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing pages on the root website (`artifacts/www`)
- Product, feature, pricing, FAQ, support, coverage, guidelines, download, privacy, and terms pages
- Crawlability files for the public website (`robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, favicon, social meta)

## Out of scope
- Authenticated customer portal routes (`/portal/**`)
- Admin routes (`/admin/**`)
- Desktop companion app (`/app/**`)
- Mobile companion app (`/field/**`)

## Target audience
- Property managers and property management companies handling nonpayment notices.
